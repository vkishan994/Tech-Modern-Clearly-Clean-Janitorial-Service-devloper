About Us

Clearly Clean Janitorial Service was founded in 2014 in response to the continual need for reliable, hard-working, honest, and reputable cleaning services in the Rio Rancho area

We are a small business, minority and service-disabled veteran owned company that is committed to providing our clients with the highest quality cleaning services possible.

We understand that the appearance of your business is important, and we take pride in our work. We use only the highest quality cleaning products and equipment, and our team members are highly trained and experienced. We also offer a variety of cleaning services to meet the needs of our clients, including:

  • Day and Night Janitorial Services
  • Covid Disinfections
  • Power spraying and Pest control
  • Commercial office Cleaning
  • Medical Cleaning
  • Government Facility Cleaning
  • Clean Room/Lab Cleaning

We are confident that Clearly Clean Janitorial Service is the best choice for your cleaning needs. While the industry might not be a glamorous one, we recognize that the quality of our work affects your appearance within your business circle; and Clearly Clean was founded on the principle that if you don't look good, we don't look good. Contact us today to learn more about our services.
